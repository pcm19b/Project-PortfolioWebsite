About Me

Hello! My name is Payton Molen. I'm a Data and Business Analyst.

My name is Payton Molen and I am a data analyst. I recently graduated from Florida State University with a Bachelor of Science in Business Marketing and Business Analytics. Through my education and experiences I find myself passionate about the opportunity to leverage data as a "storytelling" and problem solving tool in business environments, and desire to pursue Graduate level education in Data Science or Business Analytics in the future. Through my pursuit into the world of data analytics, I hope to find positions where I can leverage data storytelling to garner actionable insights that help decision makers at successful companies.

Through my education and experiences I have a strong and varied foundation in the many different facets of data analysis, including data visualization in Tableau, database creation and querying using SQL, database management in Excel, website coding in HTML, and statistical analyses using SPSS. Post graduation I continue to develop myself through complex, comprehensive projects designed to show my skills in applicable business contexts, as well as the pursuit of certification programs such as Google's Advanced Data Analyst program where I continue to learn in areas such as RStudio. The world of data and business is an exciting and evergrowing, and I look forward to contributing!
